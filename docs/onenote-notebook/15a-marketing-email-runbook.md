# Marketing — Email Runbook

Tactical playbook for every email you'll send customers and leads.
The marketing strategy lives in `15-marketing-playbook.md`; this is
the execution.

---

## Email principles

1. **Subject line is 80% of the email.** Write 5 versions, pick the best.
2. **One call to action per email.** Two CTAs = no CTAs.
3. **Mobile first.** 70%+ of opens are on phones. Short paragraphs.
4. **Personal voice.** Sound like the Owner writing to a neighbour.
5. **Send from a person, not "no-reply".** Replies go to a real inbox.

## Email program — what to send and when

### Transactional (triggered by behaviour)

| Email | When | Length | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quote sent | Immediately after quote created | Short | Confirmation + next-step clarity |
| Quote follow-up #1 | 2 business days after quote, no response | Short | Friendly nudge |
| Quote follow-up #2 | 7 business days after quote | Medium | Last touch before close as Lost |
| Booking confirmation | Immediately after booking | Short | Date, scope, what to expect |
| 24-hr reminder | Day before scheduled visit | Very short | Heads-up only |
| Invoice sent | Same day as job complete | Short | Pay link prominent |
| Payment reminder #1 | 7 days overdue | Short | Light tone |
| Payment reminder #2 | 14 days overdue | Medium | Firmer, with Office direct line |
| Review request | 2 hours after job complete (text + email) | Very short | Single CTA to Google review link |
| Thank you for review | After review posted | Very short | Genuine; no ask |

### Campaign (scheduled / seasonal)

| Email | When | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| Spring cleanup pre-sale | Mid-January | All last year's lawn customers |
| Spring lawn contract renewal | Mid-February | All recurring lawn customers from last year |
| Fall cleanup booking | Mid-August | All last year's lawn customers |
| Snow contract pre-sale | Early September | All last year's snow customers + lawn customers |
| Year-end thank you | Mid-December | Every active customer |
| Inactive customer reactivation | 6 months no contact | Customers with no recent service |
| Referral program reminder | Quarterly | Every active customer |

## Email tooling

- Send from a real human inbox (contact@ or your name@) — not a
  generic noreply
- For batch / scheduled campaigns: use the CRM's built-in email OR
  Mailchimp / SendGrid free tier (under 2,000 subs free)
- Track opens + clicks per campaign
- Unsubscribe link in every campaign email (required by CASL — Canadian
  Anti-Spam Legislation)
- CASL requires:
  - Express consent (or implied consent from existing business
    relationship within 2 years)
  - Sender identification (your name + address)
  - Unsubscribe mechanism that processes within 10 business days

Document consent for every customer — a paper trail matters if CRTC
ever asks.

## Email scorecard — review monthly

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Open rate (transactional) | 60%+ |
| Open rate (campaign) | 25%+ |
| Click rate (campaign) | 5%+ |
| Reply rate (1:1 transactional) | 30%+ |
| Unsubscribe rate per campaign | < 2% |
| Quote follow-up → close rate | 20%+ (without follow-up it drops to 5%) |

If open rate drops — subject line problem.
If click rate drops — CTA isn't clear or doesn't match expectation.
If unsubscribe rate spikes — content/cadence is off.
