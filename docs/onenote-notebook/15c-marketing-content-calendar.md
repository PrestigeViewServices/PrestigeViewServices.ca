# Marketing — 90-Day Content Calendar

Pre-filled post ideas for the next 90 days across Google Business
Profile, Facebook, and Instagram. Copy + adapt to current week's
work. Saves you 80% of the thinking — just bring the photos.

The cadence assumptions match `13-social-media.md`:
- GBP: 1 post/week (Monday)
- Facebook: 3 posts/week (Mon/Wed/Fri)
- Instagram: 3 posts/week (Mon/Wed/Fri) + 2 stories/week

Adjust to your real cadence; the IDEAS work regardless of channel.

---

## How to use this

1. First Monday of the quarter (or month), open this file
2. Pick the next 4-13 weeks of slots
3. For each slot, find the photo from your gallery / job folder
4. Adapt the caption — change names, add a real detail
5. Schedule the batch in Meta Business Suite (or your tool)
6. Done for the month in 3 hours

## Posting tips that apply to every channel

- Always include a CTA — even soft ones ("Quote: prestigeviewservices.ca")
- First 1-2 lines are critical — that's what people see before
  tapping "more"
- Tag the location (Petawawa, Pembroke, etc.) so locals find it
- Use 3-5 hashtags max, not 30
- Best posting times for the Valley: 7-8am, 12-1pm, 6-8pm
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours

---

## Quarterly content batch process

First Monday of the quarter, block 3 hours:

1. **Hour 1**: Review the prior quarter — top 3 posts, what worked,
   what didn't
2. **Hour 2**: Pick photos for the upcoming 13 weeks (from job
   folder, gallery, mascot, team)
3. **Hour 3**: Draft + schedule the next 4 weeks of posts in
   Meta Business Suite / Later / Buffer

Repeat every month: 1 hour batch for the next 4 weeks.

After 3 months you'll have a rhythm. After 12 months you'll have a
content library worth recycling.
